1. Debug the player whose rank needs to be changed.
2. Locate the `guild` list variable.
3. Open the variable and find the appropriate guild.
   - They may be part of multiple factions, so make sure you pick the right one.
4. Go to the `members` list variable.
5. Find the player you wish to change the rank within the list.
   - Members are listed by their ckey.
6. Open their var, find the `rank` value.
7. Modify the rank to the desired value.

## Rank Values

Guild ranks range from **1** to **7**.

- `1` — Lowest rank
- `7` — Highest rank (LEADER)

:::warning

Always make sure that the requested rank change is something decided preferbly ICly or OOCLY depending on the situation.

If the player belongs to multiple guilds, double-check that you are editing the correct entry.

:::
