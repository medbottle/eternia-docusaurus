## 1. Receive Influence and Money

Before beginning:

- Take the coins and influence from the leader by debugging.
- For logging purposes, **do not have the leader drop the money**.
    - Ask them to have the money in hand.
    - Debug the leader and remove the coins directly.
    - This ensures both the coin removal and influence deduction appear in our staff log.
    - Be extremely careful when entering values, especially with large numbers and extra zeros.

## 2. Record the Spawn

Add the appropriate information to `ability_tracker`, including:

- Year
- Leader name
- Soldier name
- Progress (`X/3`)
- Date
- Any other required information

Example: [May 05 2026, 13:58] Spawn a soldier - Atheleon (3/3) by Percival S. Anwar - Gretchen Hayme

---

## 3. Setting the spawn-a-soldier slot

- Reach out to the spawn-a-soldier slot. (This can be either an existing character or a new one)
- Set up the player according to the current policy. (this is based on May - 2026)

If the player is below 210:

- Update `activity_points`.
- Update `lifetime_activity`.

Add 7000 to each.

---

## 4. Classes

### Medical Class

- Medical is unlocked through the **EDIT** menu by toggling the Medical Token option.

### Faith

- Costs **5 RPP**, hand it out through **Reward Spell**.

### Sorcery

- Applies a permanent **-5** penalty, hand it out through **Reward Spell** and utilize edit to make them a custom, fluffed sorcerer's seal.

---

## 5. Create the Weapon

:::warning
Ensure you are doing this in the breakroom!
:::

1. Open **SPAWN ANYTHING**.
2. Search for **EXALTED STAFF**. (This is arcanium staff, but they may request a sword as well)
3. Spawn the weapon.
4. Open the Edit menu, make the following changes:
   - Bind it to the player by name.
   - Add the **soulbound** tag.
   - Add the **UNIQUE** tag.
   - Change the icon.
   - Change the description if any requested.
   - Change the name if any requested.

All custom details should be provided by the player.

:::tip

You can also add **Created by** and **Created Year** if you wish to give the weapon a little bit more of lore.

:::

---

## 6. Double-check at the end

Before concluding the ticket, confirm that everything is correct.

Request player to:

- Check their `/activity` EXP
- Check if they have been deducted correctly from **FAITH** or **SORCERY**
- Check their weapon if it's bound, getting EXP correctly and/or right description/name

---

:::warning

When taking a spaw-a-soldier ticket, always remember that the 10-year cooldown is based on the **first spawn** of the settlement during their cooldown, **not the most recent**.

So, if I spawned a soldier on January 1st, even if I do not use all my slots, my cooldown resets on March 12th.

:::
